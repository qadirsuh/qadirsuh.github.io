01. About Me

I started my career in 2012 as an Android developer at Genetech Solutions and spent the next decade getting really good at building native mobile apps—both Android and iOS. Along the way, I expanded into backend work with Node.js and discovered a passion for building chatbots and conversational interfaces using DialogFlow, well before it became a buzzword.

That curiosity about data and intelligence behind the apps pushed me to earn my Master's in Data Engineering from NED University. I started working with tools like Apache Airflow and n8n to build automated data pipelines and workflows. Then came the shift into Agentic AI—and everything clicked.

Today I call myself an AI-Native Engineer. It means I use AI not just as a tool, but as a core part of how I architect, build, and deliver software. With strong Prompt Engineering skills and a solid understanding of software architecture, I can parachute into any tech stack—whether that's Flutter, Python, or something else entirely—and ship production-quality work. I've successfully handled multiple clients and projects simultaneously this way.
